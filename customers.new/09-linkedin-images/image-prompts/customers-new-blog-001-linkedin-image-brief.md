# LinkedIn Image Theme Brief — customers.new (blog-001)

## Product/service summary
customers.new is an AI voice-and-text chatbot and lead-capture tool for small businesses. It studies a website, installs via a lightweight snippet, answers visitor questions around the clock, qualifies prospects, and sends captured leads to the business.

## Target audience
Small and mid-sized business owners and operators (services, trades, local and online shops) who lose high-intent website traffic and can't be available 24/7.

## Blog topic summary
"The Empty Store Problem" — why websites leak the overwhelming majority of interested visitors (cognitive load, the five-minute rule), how conversational AI plugs the leak via progressive profiling, and how to do it safely (consent + grounded, guardrailed AI).

## Visual theme direction
Friendly, approachable, small-business-oriented. Metaphors: a welcoming "open store" with someone ready to help vs. an empty counter; a conversation bubble replacing a long form; a 24/7 helpful presence catching visitors who'd otherwise leave.

## Mood and tone
Warm, reassuring, practical, optimistic, human. Simple and uncluttered — not enterprise-cold, not anxious.

## Suggested color direction
Inferred from the brand's friendly, approachable, conversion-focused feel (not confirmed exact brand hex): bright, warm, inviting palette — clean white base with a friendly primary (blue or green) and a warm accent (coral/amber). Treat as direction, not brand-exact.

## Suggested composition
Horizontal 1.91:1 (1200x627). Bright and welcoming, simple focal concept, generous space for a headline.

## What the image should communicate
A friendly, always-available helper turning passing visitors into conversations — approachable and trustworthy.

## What to avoid
No invented logos, no real chatbot UI/screens of customers.new, no customer names, no metrics, no certifications. Avoid generating an actual logo. Avoid readable text and long messages inside chat bubbles. Avoid cold/corporate or surveillance vibes; keep it warm and small-business friendly.

## Text handling
Avoid readable text inside the image (including legible chat-bubble text). Leave clean space for a headline/CTA to be added manually later.
