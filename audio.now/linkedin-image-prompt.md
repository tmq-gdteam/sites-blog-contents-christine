# LinkedIn Image Prompt — "The Dangerous Convenience of Voice-Driven Work"

## Image Objective
A clean, editorial hero image for a LinkedIn feed post about voice-driven work — its speed and ergonomic upside, and its quieter cognitive, security, and privacy trade-offs. The image should feel like the lead visual of a thoughtful article, supporting the post without depicting any product UI or readable text.

## Product / Service Context
Accompanies content about Audio.Now, an ambient audio interface (read-aloud, real-time transcription, voice navigation) with a privacy-first, user-controlled voiceprint. Keep the imagery conceptual and brand-neutral — no logos, no interface, no product specifics.

## Audience Context
Productivity-minded but security- and privacy-aware professionals (knowledge workers, writers, and regulated-industry users). Visual tone must read as credible and premium, not consumer-gadget advertising.

## Visual Style
Modern conceptual editorial illustration / refined 3D-render aesthetic. Minimal, premium, lots of negative space. Soft, even studio lighting with a single subtle focal glow. Clean and uncluttered.

## Composition
Landscape orientation. A single focal point — the origin of a voice/sound signal — placed off-center on a rule-of-thirds intersection. Concentric audio ripples or a smooth waveform flow outward from it toward a faint structural boundary (a subtle grid, frame, or threshold line) at one edge, suggesting an open signal meeting a quiet limit. Generous calm negative space on the opposite side for optional later headline overlay.

## Color / Mood Guidance
Cool, trustworthy, low-saturation palette: deep navy or charcoal background, a soft teal / muted electric-blue accent for the audio elements, and neutral off-white or pale-grey negative space. Calm, intelligent, slightly contemplative mood with a faint sense of tension between openness and boundary. (No confirmed brand colors — adjust if official colors become available.)

## Subject Matter
Abstract representation of voice and sound: glowing concentric ripples / a flowing soundwave emanating from a small luminous point, drifting toward a subtle grid or edge that hints at structure and limits. Metaphorical, not literal — no people required, no devices in focus.

## Restrictions
- No logos, brand marks, or wordmarks.
- No user interface, dashboards, app screens, or device mockups.
- No certifications, badges, awards, charts, or metrics.
- No real or implied customer faces, names, or testimonials.
- No literal attack imagery (e.g., lasers hacking devices).
- No long or readable text anywhere in the image.
- Avoid stock-photo clichés (call-center headsets) and robotic/dystopian tropes.

## Negative Prompt
text, words, letters, captions, watermark, logo, brand name, UI, app screen, dashboard, buttons, charts, graphs, numbers, certification badges, human faces, hands holding phone, headset, robot, cluttered composition, busy background, low resolution, distorted, oversaturated, neon overload, clip-art, meme

## Recommended Aspect Ratio
1200×627 (1.91:1) — LinkedIn feed image.

## Text Note
Do not ask the image model to render readable text. If a headline is desired, add it manually after generation over the negative-space area.
